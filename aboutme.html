ABOUT

Me

Vanya Rashev (Bulgarian, b.1988, Stara Zagora, Bulgaria) is an upcoming digital content creator. She moved seven years ago to chase a dream of achieving a degree in digital arts. The artist is interested in history, art, culture, spirituality, and psychology in different nations and cultures. Vanya explores the possibilities of physical art, hand-drawn on paper and digital art, using various software such as Adobe Suite, Clip art Studio, and Blender. Also, coding art using HTML, CSS, and Java. Vanya participated in the digital exhibition at SJSU 2021, called "Digital Autonomy", her piece was called "Speculum Vanitas," with a 3D sculpture projecting digitally drawn animation of a blooming rose transforming into a face and then a skull. The artwork explores social impact of social media on one's self-image, self-esteem, etc.
